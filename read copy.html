Hundreds of thousands of books exist in the public domain. Most are tricky to navigate and even more difficult to read online. Our goal is to clean up all the noise and provide a selection of wonderful works. Our interface is meant to stay out of the way so you can focus on reading and nothing else. Our current read is The Metamorphisis by Franz Kafka. We deliberatly launched with one book so you can try out our product. We think it's a book everyone should read and it's a quick read. As we add more books we want feedback/input. We hope you enjoy our new product.
